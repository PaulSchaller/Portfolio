About Me

I am a web developer. I enjoy creating websites that serve functions of communicating with, informing, and conducting business with users. The internet field is growing so rapidly nowadays. I love that. I will turn on CNBC and hear the technology news about amazon, facebook, Microsoft, Google, and Apple and be excited that I am part of this field.

In high school, I was very good in math and scored 35/36 on the math section of my ACT. Clerical job positions would continually be suggested to me from psychology tests that gauged interests, because I like to organize and file. Computer programming is a good fit for me.

My education is a particular strong point. I have a business school degree, a Master's Degree in computers, and a certificate in web development. I have completed additional courses in computer programming and computer science. With my Master's Degree, I graduated with honors.

I keep current with news and culture. I have ideas of how a website could be developed to tap into business and communication opportunities with internet users. I will surf the internet and quickly notice how a website is structured and operating. I love the mechanics of the web development field.

I am looking to use my skills, interests, and work experience in a great job opportunity.
